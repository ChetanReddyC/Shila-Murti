Component Architecture:Base Layer ($T_{base}$): A static texture of the subject in low-key/shadow lighting.Emission/Rim Layer ($T_{light}$): A matching texture containing only the high-intensity highlights and rim lights (Alpha-masked).Mask ($M$): A dynamic, feathered linear gradient.2.
